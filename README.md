# **Hamak Newspaper - Digital Platform**

## **Overview**

Hamak Newspaper is a powerful **Digital News & Interactive Platform** designed to manage and oversee journalistic operations efficiently. The system allows administrators to control various aspects, including **news distribution, community engagement, multimedia libraries, interactive quizzes, and e-commerce**.

This platform serves as the **backbone** of a modern media portal, enabling teams to manage content and community features in a structured and organized way. The system ensures a **smooth and efficient workflow** by providing real-time publishing and interactive tools for admins and readers alike.

## **Key Features**

### **1. Comprehensive News Coverage**

* Continuous and unbiased coverage of local, Arab, and international news.
* Organize and categorize breaking news and investigative reports dynamically.
* Maintain a **centralized database of journalistic content**.

### **2. Citizen Journalism ("You Are the Eyewitness")**

* Empower the audience to be an active part of news-making.
* Allow users to share events via photos and videos directly from the field.
* Assign roles and permissions for content review and approval.

### **3. Hamak Al-Khair (Humanitarian Initiatives)**

* Interactive social and humanitarian initiatives module.
* Provide a structured platform to extend a helping hand and deliver aid to those in need.
* Track initiative performance and community contributions in real time.

### **4. Hamak Audio Library**

* Listen to news reports, live broadcasts, and various radio programs.
* Support for exclusive podcasts and browsing authors' works with excellent audio quality.
* Manage system audio tracks, uploads, and metadata seamlessly.

### **5. Interactive Quizzes & Competitions**

* Test user knowledge with diverse questions across cultural and scientific fields.
* Renewable interactive challenges designed to increase the audience's knowledge base.
* Generate reports on user engagement and leaderboard metrics.

### **6. Upgraded Hamak Marketplace**

* An integrated e-commerce marketplace allowing users to sell products for free.
* Upload products easily, share them with friends, and communicate directly with customers safely.
* Secure system for listing management and role-based fraud control.

### **7. Discussion Forums**

* Interactive discussion spaces that bring readers together to exchange views.
* Publish and manage important topics and current events for community debate.
* Data protection and moderation tools to ensure community integrity.

### **8. Full Responsiveness & Device Support**

* Fully responsive and fast browsing experience supporting various systems and smart devices.
* Monitor traffic trends across different screen sizes and device types.
* Optimized interface tailored for both desktop and mobile users.

## **Why Choose Hamak Newspaper Platform?**

* **Scalable & Reliable:** Handles multiple users, heavy concurrent traffic, and rich multimedia content.
* **User-Friendly Admin Panel:** Simple and intuitive interface for news editing, market management, and forum moderation.
* **Customizable:** Easily add new categories, quiz types, and community features as needed.
* **Real-Time Functionality:** Instant news updates, real-time notifications, and live chat for the marketplace.

## **Technology Stack**

Hamak Newspaper Platform is built using a modern tech stack to ensure performance and scalability:

* **Backend:** Laravel (PHP Framework)
* **Frontend:** Blade Templates / Vue.js / Tailwind CSS
* **Database:** MySQL / PostgreSQL
* **Authentication:** Laravel Passport / Sanctum
* **Notifications:** Firebase / WebSockets / Laravel Notifications
